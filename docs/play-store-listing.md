## Full Description (4000 chars max)

DoseWise is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

DoseWise is a beautifully designed medication reminder app that helps you and your family stay on top of medication schedules. Whether you're managing your own prescriptions, tracking vitamins, or caring for a family member's health routine — DoseWise makes it simple and reliable.

💊 SIMPLE MEDICATION MANAGEMENT
• Add medications with name, dosage, form (tablet, capsule, liquid, and more)
• Set daily or specific-day schedules with custom times
• Track pill stock and get refill reminders before you run out
• Edit any medication detail at any time — no restrictions

⏰ RELIABLE REMINDERS
• Alarm-style notifications that work even on silent mode
• Take or Snooze directly from the notification
• Never miss a dose with high-priority alerts
• Smart scheduling for morning, noon, evening, and night doses

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 FAMILY CARE
• Create separate profiles for family members
• Track medications for parents, grandparents, spouse, or children
• Switch between profiles with one tap
• Each profile has its own medications, schedules, and history

📊 TRACK YOUR PROGRESS
• Daily progress circle shows doses taken vs. scheduled
• Weekly adherence percentage on your schedule view
• Per-medication adherence stats
• Visual status for taken, skipped, and pending doses

🔒 PRIVATE & SECURE
• All data stored locally on your device — never uploaded anywhere
• No accounts, no servers, no tracking
• Optional biometric lock and passcode protection
• Your health data stays with you, always

✨ BEAUTIFUL DESIGN
• Clean, modern interface that feels premium
• Smooth animations and haptic feedback
• Easy-to-read medication cards with clear status indicators
• Designed for all ages — simple enough for everyone

FREE FEATURES:

✓ Dose tracking & adherence stats
✓ Refill reminders
✓ Biometric security

PREMIUM FEATURES:
