Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 24, 2026

DoseWise ("we," "our," or "the app") is a medication reminder and tracker application. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Medical Disclaimer: DoseWise is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

1. Data We Collect

DoseWise collects and stores the following types of information:

  • Medication Information: Names, dosages, schedules, and forms of medications you enter
  • Dose Tracking: Records of when you take, skip, or miss doses
  • Profile Information: Name, age, blood type (all optional)
  • App Preferences: Notification settings, theme preferences, unit preferences

2. How Data is Stored

All your data is stored locally on your device only. We do not have servers, databases, cloud storage, or any remote infrastructure. Your medication data never leaves your phone.

  • Data is stored using your device's local storage (AsyncStorage)
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • No data is synced to any cloud service
  • No backups are created on external servers

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone. Since all data is stored locally on your device, we have no access to it and cannot share it with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These services may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data according to their own privacy policies. Premium users can remove all advertisements.

5. Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features. The app offers optional biometric authentication (fingerprint) and passcode lock for additional security. Since no data leaves your device, there is no risk of server-side data breaches.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data persists on your device until you:

  • Delete individual medications or profiles within the app
  • Use the "Log Out" feature to clear all app data
  • Clear the app's storage from your device settings
  • Uninstall the app (all data is permanently deleted)

7. Children's Privacy

DoseWise is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for adults managing their own or their family members' medication schedules.

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your data at all times. You can view, edit, or delete any data within the app. No request to us is needed since we never have access to your data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated effective date.
